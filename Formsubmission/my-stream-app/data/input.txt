Dubai is a city and emirate in the United Arab Emirates known for luxury shopping, ultramodern architecture and a lively nightlife scene. Burj Khalifa, an 830m-tall tower, dominates the skyscraper-filled skyline. At its foot lies Dubai Fountain, with jets and lights choreographed to music. On artificial islands just offshore is Atlantis, The Palm, a resort with water and marine-animal parks.

Qatar is a peninsular Arab country whose terrain comprises arid desert and a long Persian (Arab) Gulf shoreline of beaches and dunes. Also on the coast is the capital, Doha, known for its futuristic skyscrapers and other ultramodern architecture inspired by ancient Islamic design, such as the limestone Museum of Islamic Art. The museum sits on the city’s Corniche waterfront promenade.

China, officially the People's Republic of China, is a major country in East Asia with a population over 1.4 billion, making it one of the world's most populous nations. It is a global hub for manufacturing and exporting, recognized as an emerging superpower with significant influence in economics, technology, and geopolitics.


Location and Geography: Located in East Asia, it is a vast country featuring diverse landscapes, including the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region and scenic areas like Bipenggou.
Government: Since 1949, it has been a unitary communist state governed by the Communist Party of China (CCP).
Economy: China is the world's second-wealthiest country and holds the largest economy when adjusted for purchasing power parity (PPP).
Culture and Tourism: Known for its ancient culture, it features 60 UNESCO World Heritage Sites, including the Songzanlin Monastery.

The official language of China is Mandarin (Putonghua), the most spoken language globally, acting as the national lingua franca for education, media, and government. While Mandarin is based on the Beijing dialect, China possesses immense linguistic diversity with hundreds of local dialects and seven major language groups (including Cantonese, Wu, and Min), all using shared Chinese characters (logograms).
